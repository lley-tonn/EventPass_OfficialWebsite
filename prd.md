# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
# EventPass Landing Page v2
### Version: 1.0
### Status: Design Specification
### Project: EventPass (CinQ)
### Goal: Build the definitive landing page for Uganda's leading event ecosystem.

---

# Vision

This landing page is **not** a website.

It is the digital identity of EventPass.

When someone lands on this page they should immediately understand that EventPass is:

- The easiest way to discover events.
- The smartest way to organize events.
- The fastest way to buy tickets.
- The most trusted ticketing ecosystem in Uganda.

The landing page should feel like Apple meets Stripe meets Linear, with the excitement of Tomorrowland.

The page should not feel like a template.

It should feel like a premium digital product.

---

# Brand Positioning

EventPass is **not** a ticket seller.

EventPass powers experiences.

We are building the operating system for events.

The product serves two equally important audiences.

## Attendees

People who want to

- Discover events
- Buy tickets
- Store tickets
- Scan QR codes
- Follow organizers
- Receive recommendations

---

## Organizers

People who want to

- Create events
- Sell tickets
- Track sales
- Scan attendees
- View analytics
- Grow audiences

The landing page must communicate both experiences without making either feel secondary.

# Success Metrics

The landing page should achieve

- Immediate product recognition in under 3 seconds.
- Strong visual identity that is memorable after one visit.
- High trust through authentic social proof and platform statistics.
- Clear communication to both attendees and organizers.
- Excellent performance, accessibility, and SEO.
- Premium aesthetic without sacrificing usability.

---

# Final Design Principle

Every pixel should answer one question:

**"Does this make EventPass feel like the platform that powers every unforgettable event?"**

If the answer is no, it doesn't belong on the page.
